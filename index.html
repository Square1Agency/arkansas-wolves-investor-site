The Opportunity

Little Rock has a population, an income base, and a downtown revitalization story that fits the metro profile every U.S. pro soccer league is looking for. It does not yet have a pro soccer team. The Wolves have spent six years earning the right to be that team — twelve consecutive top-four UPSL finishes, the first Arkansas club to host UPSL regionals, and the state's largest youth academy.

What we are raising for is the next chapter: a venue, an ownership group, and the operating runway to turn a state's most successful soccer organization into a generational community asset.
